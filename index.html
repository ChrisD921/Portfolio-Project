About Me

I'm a Filipino American voice actor with a baritone voice suited for Teen, Young Adult, and Adult male roles. My work spans video games, audio dramas, and animation — including playing Lucius Ryker in Starkheim Tournament, a 2D pixel art fighting game now in Early Access on Steam, and leading roles across several narrative-driven audio drama series. I trained in Musical Theater, Improv, and Student Films before moving into voice acting, and continue to sharpen my craft through dedicated workshops in character work, video game performance, and accents/dialects. If you're looking for a Baritone Voice for Young Adult or Adult roles, you've found your man!
