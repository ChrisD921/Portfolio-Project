About Me

I have had a passion for voice acting and voice over for quite a few years. Over the last couple years, I have been able to improve my skills and gather knowledge about the industry. Every day, I am grateful for the opportunity to grow and practice in a craft I love.

I am an experienced actor, having tried my hand at Musical Theater, Improv Sketches, as well as Student Films. I have been able to establish a small, but constantly growing, portfolio of voice work in my time in the industry. If you are looking for someone with a Baritone range for Young Adult or Adult roles, you've found your man!
